Creamy and flavorful Indian butter chicken, perfect with rice or naan.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast, diced
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 can tomato puree
  • 100ml cream
  • Butter, salt, pepper

Instructions

  1. Fry onions, garlic, and ginger in butter.
  2. Add chicken and spices, cook until browned.
  3. Add tomato puree, simmer 20 minutes.
  4. Stir in cream, heat through, and serve.
