A spicy and hearty Indian chickpea curry, perfect with rice or naan.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans chickpeas, drained
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • Salt and chili to taste

Instructions

  1. Saut√© onion, garlic, and ginger until soft.
  2. Add spices and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Add chickpeas and simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with rice or naan.
