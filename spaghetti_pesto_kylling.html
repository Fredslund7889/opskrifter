A classic Italian pasta dish featuring silky eggs, crispy pancetta, and plenty of freshly grated Parmesan cheese. This authentic carbonara recipe is creamy without using cream!

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 100g Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt for pasta water
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Cook spaghetti according to package directions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. While pasta cooks, heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add diced pancetta and cook until crispy, about 5-7 minutes. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 more minute.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Parmesan, and a generous amount of black pepper.
  4. Add the drained hot pasta to the skillet with pancetta. Remove from heat.
  5. Quickly pour the egg mixture over the pasta, tossing continuously to create a creamy sauce. Add pasta water gradually if needed to achieve desired consistency.
  6. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan cheese and black pepper. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
